Smart Media Buying

For Clients & Media Buyers

With our platform, you're in full control:

  • Real-time access to site availability
  • Reserve inventory instantly
  • Negotiate rates live with site owners
  • Book directly—no middlemen, no hassle
  • Download automated schedules and chase lists effortlessly
  • One invoice, multiple media owners—easy and consolidated
  • Upload artwork for seamless flighting
  • Get proof of flighting directly from media owners
  • Accurate procurement tracking for empowerment spending

For Media Owners

Supercharge your business and boost visibility:

  • Track business performance in real-time
  • Monitor sales activities as they happen
  • Showcase your sites with videos and images
  • Set pre-negotiated rates for each inventory
  • Create seasonal promo packages to attract buyers
  • Respond instantly to inventory requests
  • Negotiate rates live with media buyers/planners
  • Download client artwork effortlessly
  • Upload proof of flighting for transparency
  • Give buyers in-depth insights about site locations
